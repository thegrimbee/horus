User Guide

Here is a user guide for Horus:

1. Select an app:
- Click on the "Select an app" dropdown button to choose an app.
- The available apps will be listed in the dropdown menu.

2. Scan an app:
- Click on the "Scan" button to scan the selected app.
- The scanning process will start, and the progress will be shown in the loading bar.

3. Scan all apps (DO NOT CLICK THIS BUTTON AT ALL COSTS):
- Click on the "Scan All" button to scan all the available apps.
- The scanning process will start for each app, and the progress will be shown in the loading bar.

4. View scanned apps:
- The scanned apps will be displayed in the "Apps scanned" section.
- You can scroll through the list to view all the scanned apps.

5. Filter results by level:
- Use the level buttons (Danger, Warning, Normal) to filter the results based on their severity level.
- Click on the desired level button to show only the results of that level.

6. View results:
- The results will be displayed in the "Here are the results" section.
- You can scroll through the results to view all the details.

7. View alternative apps:
- Click on the "Alternative Apps" button to open the modal window.
- The modal window will show a list of alternative apps for different categories.
- Click on the category buttons to expand and view the list of apps in each category.

8. Close the user guide:
- Click on the "Close" button in the user guide modal to close it.
